community guidelines

A few simple habits make every transaction smoother. Follow these four principles to build trust, attract serious buyers, and keep our marketplace friendly for everyone.

Clear, honest descriptions

List every detail that matters — size, brand, condition, and any flaws. When you describe an item exactly as it is, buyers feel confident and returns drop. Think of your listing as the first handshake.

Real condition notes

Use the condition scale honestly. Upload well-lit photos from multiple angles and show any wear up close. Buyers browsing items for sale on Facebook Marketplace near you will appreciate the transparency.

Respectful communication

Reply promptly and politely. Agree on price, pickup location, and payment method before meeting. Clear chat threads keep both sides comfortable — whether you are selling on Facebook Marketplace or right here on J-SET.
